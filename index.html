ROADMAP

The World You’ve been waiting for

01

10,000 digital waterfront mansions on plots of land consisting of 100 variations will mint. Everyone who mints will receive a virtual mansion and plot of land that serves as a governance token which allows them to participate in governance decisions on the platform using a DAO structure. Our ERC-20 token will launch and token holders will be able to stake their NFTs.

02

Once the initial mint is concluded, the first virtual event will take place with the speaker to be announced along with our full gold paper. Land development continues and we will collaborate with the community for input of hubs and release a second collection of highrise units.

03

We will roll out meta access to token holders residences where they can hangout with their friends and other community members. We will host our second virtual event, this one being in the metaverse. We will also release our first 2 hubs and drop our 3rd mint consisting of mansions.

04

Avatar creation begins for token holders and we will release another hub. We will give away a vehicle with the release of vehicles in our metaverse and an alpha version is dropped. The 4th mint will then take place and the remaining mansions and landholders will be assigned. We will host our third virtual event.

05

The final mint of residential plots takes place. Avatar creation services are available to the public. Fourth virtual event is held.

06

Commercial land is sold. Remainder of hubs are released. Avatar creation for token holders is completed. Fifth virtual event is held.
